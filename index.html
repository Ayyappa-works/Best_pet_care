Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring for my pet's appointment?

For veterinary appointments, please bring any previous medical records and a list of current medications. For grooming, training, or boarding, you can bring your pet's favorite toys, bedding, and any special dietary food.

Do I need to make an appointment for services?

Yes, appointments are required for all services to ensure we can provide the best care for your pet. You can easily schedule an appointment through our website or by calling us.

What types of pets do you care for?

We primarily care for dogs and cats, but we also offer services for small animals like rabbits, guinea pigs, and birds. Please contact us for more information on caring for other types of pets.

What if my pet has special needs or medical conditions?

Our team is experienced in handling pets with special needs or medical conditions. Please provide detailed information about your pet's requirements when making an appointment so we can ensure proper care.

How do you ensure the safety and comfort of my pet during their stay?

Your pet’s safety and comfort are our top priorities. Our facilities are designed to be safe, clean, and comfortable. We monitor all pets closely and provide plenty of attention, exercise, and affection.
