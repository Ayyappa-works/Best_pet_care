About the Product

Taiyo Pluss Discovery special fish food is a balanced and staple diet for all tropical fishes. It is scientifically developed to meet the basic dietary needs for healthy growth and lustrous skin. This protein rich food accelerates the growth rate of the fish due to presence of natural ingredients that outstandingly increase the appetite and keep the fish in decent shape. This feed is produced using advanced extrusion process that ensures maximum palatability and additionally helps prevent water contamination.

Composition

Freeze Dried Brine Shrimp, Fish meal, Fish protein concentrate, Wheat Flour, Wheat Gluten meal, Tapioca, Dried Yeast, Fish Liver meal, Salmon Oil, Dried Kelp, Scallop meal, Soy Protein concentrate, Cochineal extract, Vitamins ( ( Cholecalciferol, Biotin, d-Calcium Pantoethenate, Folic acid, Inositol, Niacin supplement, Riboflavin-5-Phosphate, Calcium L-Ascorbyl-2-Monophosphate, Vitamin A Acetate, Thiamine Hydrochloride, Vitamin B12 supplement, Pyridoxine Hydrochloride, Vitamin E supplement, Menadione Sodium Bisulfite Complex ( source of Vitamin K activity )), Yeast extract, Fructooligosaccharide, Hydrolyzed Soy protein, Lecithin, Citric Acid.

Nutritional Facts

Crude Protein 32%, Crude Fat 4%, Crude Fibre 5%, Moisture 10%

How to Use

At each feeding, feed as much as the fish can consume in about 5 minutes. Do not over feed as the fish tend to overeat and this might cause digestive problems.
