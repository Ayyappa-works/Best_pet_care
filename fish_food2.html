About the Product

Optimum Tropical Fish Food is suitable for all aquarium fish species such as goldfish, fancy carp, swordtails, platies, mollies, angelfish, cichlids, anabantids and bettas. Contains Astaxanthin enhances the brilliant natural color of aquarium fish. Rich in vitamin C and vitamin E to help reduce stress and increase immunity . Complete nutrition. Non - Water fouling.

Ingredients

Fish Meal, Shrimp or Shrimp by Product. Corn Protein Meal. Soybean Meal, Corn Cassava Meal, Fish Oil, Lecithin, Vitamins & minerals, Astaxanthin (Colour Enhance), Food Coloring, Antioxidants.

How to Use

Feed two to three times daily. Sprinkle just enough food that the fish can consume within 15 minutes. Do not overfeed because unconsumed food will reduce overall water quality (to the detriment of your fish).
